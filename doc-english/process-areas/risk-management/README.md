# ⚠️ Process Area — Risk Management

> This folder is **self-contained**: everything you need to understand the area is here.

## 📌 What this area is in MPS.BR
Risk identification, analysis and response. Maps to **Risk Management (GRI)**.

## 🎯 What problem it solves
Solves **being caught off guard by avoidable problems**.

## ✅ What belongs in this area
- Risk identification and analysis
- Risk response plan
- Risk tracking

## 🚫 What does NOT belong in this area
- Schedule (→ projects)
- General decisions (→ decisions)
