# General Review Checklist

## Objective

Provide a general verification list for work product reviews.

---

## Documentation

- [ ] Documents updated.
- [ ] Consistent information.
- [ ] Evidence recorded.

---

## Product

- [ ] Acceptance criteria met.
- [ ] Critical defects resolved.
- [ ] Requirements implemented correctly.

---

## Process

- [ ] Defined procedures were followed.
- [ ] Specific checklists were executed.
- [ ] Necessary approvals were obtained.

---

## Configuration

- [ ] Items versioned correctly.
- [ ] Baselines updated.
- [ ] Changes recorded.

---

## Review Result

- [ ] Approved
- [ ] Approved with remarks
- [ ] Rejected
