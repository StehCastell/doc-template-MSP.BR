# 🎚️ MPS.BR Level G — Partially Managed

> First management controls: requirements and project.

## 📖 Definition
Level G starts maturity by managing requirements and planning/monitoring the project. It is the basis for predictability.

## 🚪 Entry criteria
- Management commitment to planning.

## 🏁 Exit criteria
- Managed requirements and a monitored project.
