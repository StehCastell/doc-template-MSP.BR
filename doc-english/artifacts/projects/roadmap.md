# Prioritization Criteria

Features shall be prioritized considering:

1. Value to the user.
2. Business impact.
3. Technical dependencies.
4. Implementation complexity.
5. Risks involved.

---

# Roadmap Review

The roadmap shall be reviewed:

* At the end of each release.
* When there are significant scope changes.
* When new requirements are approved.
